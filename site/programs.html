Explore Our Programs

Whether you’re just starting your Scouting journey or looking for new challenges, the Pony Express Council offers programs for all ages and interests. Learn more about what each program has to offer.

Cub Scouts (Ages 5–10)

A family-oriented program introducing young Scouts to values through fun, hands-on activities guided by adult volunteers and parents.

  • Rank Structure: Tigers, Wolves, Bears, Webelos, Arrow of Light.
  • Family Involvement: Parents join meetings, outings, and service projects.
  • Outdoor Adventures: Nature hikes, camping overnights, and wildlife exploration.
  • STEM & Skills: Hands-on experiments, crafts, and belt loops in science, tech, engineering, and math.

Scouts BSA (Ages 11–17)

The traditional outdoor program empowering youth to lead and grow through the Patrol Method, advancing from Scout through Eagle.

  • Youth-Led Troops: Scouts elect leaders and plan activities.
  • Merit Badges: Over 135 badges—from First Aid to Robotics.
  • Outdoor Skills: Camping, backpacking, aquatics, high-adventure trips.
  • Leadership & Service: Lead projects, mentor younger Scouts, community service.
  • Rank to Eagle: Leadership, merit badges, and a service project culminate in Eagle.

Venturing (Ages 14–20)

A high-adventure program for older teens and young adults focused on leadership, personal growth, and teamwork through exciting activities.

  • Crew Leadership: Officers manage crew operations.
  • High-Adventures: Sailing, climbing, expeditions, and service trips.
  • Personal Development: Bronze, Gold, and Ranger Awards for skill mastery.
  • Service & Mentoring: Partner with community orgs and mentor younger Scouts.
  • Open to All: No prior Scouting experience required.
